## 3. Boards & Mats

1.  **Squirrel Player Mat**: Tracks building costs, harvest dice rules.
2.  **Fox Player Mat**: Tracks building costs, exploration stats (N/M), wall/shield rules.
3.  **Owl Player Mat**: Tracks building costs, research limit, display size (N).
4.  **Badger Player Mat**: Tracks Great Work info, Lead action costs.
5.  **Badger Worker Placement Board**: Spaces for Construct, Train, Ensure Quality, Install, Commission.
6.  **Tech & Magic Tree Board**: Tracks for Knowledge/Insight and slots for unlocked tech cards/markers.
7.  **Threat/Storm Track Board**: Track with 15 spaces (1-6: Draw 1, 7-12: Draw 2, 13-14: Draw 3, 15: Loss).
