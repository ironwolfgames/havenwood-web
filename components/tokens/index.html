Printing Information

Suggested Print Quantities:

• Resource Tokens (Food, Wood, Ore, Stone, Lumber, Steel, Stone Brick): 30 of each

• House Markers: 1 of each (4 total)

• Fox Walls: 4 of each level (12 total)

• Fortifications: 4 of each level (12 total)

• Magic Shield: 6 tokens

• Blocked Token: 6 tokens

• Bandit Token: 6 tokens

• Knowledge & Insight Trackers: 1 of each

• Storm Marker: 1 token

• Round Marker: 1 token

Print Settings: All components are designed at 300 DPI. For best results, print on cardstock and optionally laminate for durability. Small tokens (0.75") work well with standard 1" circle punches after printing slightly larger.
