About Harvest Dice

Quantity Required: 10 dice

Purpose: Used by the Squirrel faction for their Harvest action, allowing push-your-luck resource gathering.

Face Distribution

  • 1 (one face): Gain 1 resource
  • 2 (one face): Gain 2 resources
  • 3 (one face): Gain 3 resources
  • BUST (two faces): Dice cannot be locked, reroll ends the harvest
  • Blank (one face): Worth 0 but can be locked

Gameplay

Players roll N dice (N = building level), lock non-bust dice, then choose to stop or reroll unlocked dice. If a bust is rolled, all unlocked dice are lost. The total value of all locked dice is gained as resources.
